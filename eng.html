About Us

FirstMotors is a modern car dealership offering carefully inspected European vehicles with transparent history and favorable purchase conditions.

We offer:

  • • a wide selection of cars available in stock;
  • • sourcing any make or model on request from Europe;
  • • a fair and advantageous trade-in — your car as part of the payment for a new one;
  • • financing and leasing through trusted partners;
  • • full transaction support: diagnostics, delivery, and paperwork;
  • • professional consignment sale of your vehicle through our platform.

We work for those who appreciate quality, efficiency, and confidence at every stage of the buying process.
There are no hidden conditions — only fair prices, real cars, and a genuinely professional approach.

Welcome to a place where cars are chosen as thoughtfully as people choose their lifestyle.

Consignment sale — how it works:

01

You bring your car to us.

You arrive at any time convenient for you. We perform an exterior inspection, check the condition, discuss the possible market price, and answer all your questions.

02

We agree on the terms and sign a consignment contract.

We set the price, the timeframe, and the commission amount. No hidden fees or obligations — only clear and transparent terms.

03

We take full responsibility for preparing and selling your car.

Your vehicle stays on our lot. We handle viewings, communication with buyers, negotiations, document preparation, and all related matters. You don’t have to spend time on calls or meetings.

04

When the car is sold — you receive the money.

After the deal is completed, you receive the agreed amount in the way that’s most convenient for you. Fast, safe, and hassle-free.
